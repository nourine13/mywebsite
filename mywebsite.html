" The One Who Sold The World "

They said teenagers are the future of any era, but what if these teenagers considered as a biggest threat to the world? Later on, something unpredictable happened to this group but they managed to keep up. As a result of all these events, new groups have born, each group has one goal is to be “The Emperor of The World” Who are they? What’s the secret behind the twins? And what happened to “Heaven’s Wings”? Interesting events await in this story
